Privacy & Disclosures

Gorgon Clock is a fan-made tool for the MMO Project Gorgon. This page explains what data the site stores and where it goes.

What's stored locally (your browser)

The site uses localStorage on your device to keep settings and history between visits. Specifically:

  • user_id — a random pseudonymous identifier (e.g. u_a8f31c2b), generated on first visit. Never linked to your name, email, or PG character.
  • bankroll — the council balance you've entered (used only to compute Kelly bet recommendations on your device).
  • tips, voted_slots, voted_tips, history — your collected Hot Tips, the matchups you've voted for, and the bets you've logged.

This data is "strictly necessary" for the tool to function. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

What's sent to our server

The arena copilot uses a tiny shared backend (Cloudflare Worker) so that one player's matchup entry is visible to others. When you submit a matchup, vote, or submit/upvote/remove a tip, your user_id is sent to the Worker along with the action. No other information is transmitted.

Server-side data lives in Cloudflare's KV store and auto-expires 24 hours after the relevant fight or tip-day. There is no long-term retention.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, no tracking, no cookies (only functional localStorage).
  • No third-party scripts beyond Google Fonts (which the Google privacy policy covers).
  • No personal data is ever requested or stored.
